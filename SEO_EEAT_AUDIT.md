# SS Roofing Specialist Limited

SEO, Local SEO, and E-E-A-T Audit

Date: 2026-03-31

## Executive Summary

The site has a solid technical and structural base for a local roofing business, including service pages, blog content, internal linking, sitemap coverage, and local business signals tied to Hayes, Middlesex.

The strongest improvements made in this audit pass are:

- stronger internal linking between blog posts, service pages, and conversion pages
- dedicated service detail pages instead of one broad services list only
- richer page-level schema and breadcrumb support
- clearer trust and authorship signals across key pages
- stronger local keyword targeting mapped to page intent

The site is not yet a finished “authority” site in the real-world E-E-A-T sense because genuine business proof still needs to be added before launch, especially:

- real company registration number
- real accreditations and certifications
- real project photography
- real reviews and testimonials
- stronger evidence of experience and years in trade

## Current Technical Audit

### Strengths

- crawlable page structure
- clean React routing with dedicated pages
- XML sitemap present
- internal linking is now much stronger
- page-level title and meta description handling
- Open Graph and canonical support
- LocalBusiness-style schema signals on the homepage
- service detail pages and blog pages now have more search-specific intent

### Current Technical Constraints

- the site is intentionally set to `noindex, nofollow` for staging
- `robots.txt` currently disallows crawling because the site is on a temporary domain
- official production domain values are still used in some metadata while the temporary domain is live

### Technical Priority Actions Before Public Launch

1. Remove `noindex, nofollow` from `client/index.html`
2. Change `client/public/robots.txt` to allow crawling
3. Ensure canonical URLs reflect the final live domain
4. Confirm the final domain is submitted to Google Search Console

## E-E-A-T Audit

Google’s systems do not use E-E-A-T as a single ranking factor, but trust-related signals are especially important for service businesses where decisions affect property, cost, and safety.

### What Is Stronger Now

- the site clearly identifies the business name and location
- the About page gives more context on who is behind the business
- service pages now describe specific service intent, not only generic marketing copy
- blog content is internally connected to services and conversion paths
- content review / authorship presentation has been added

### What Still Needs Real Business Evidence

- Companies House registration number
- named leadership or expert reviewer profiles
- real insurance wording
- accreditations such as trade memberships or safety/compliance certifications
- actual completed project case studies with locations, scope, and results
- genuine review sources

### Recommended E-E-A-T Additions

1. Add the real company registration number site-wide
2. Add a “Why Trust Us” page or section with real credentials
3. Add real project case studies with before/after photography
4. Add named reviewer profiles for advice content
5. Add real review excerpts and source attribution where possible

## Keyword Strategy

This strategy is intent-based and locally focused. It is designed to avoid thin keyword stuffing and instead align page purpose with likely commercial search behavior.

### Cluster 1: Primary Local Commercial Intent

Target pages:

- Home page
- Contact page

Target keyword themes:

- roofing company Hayes
- roofer Hayes
- roofing contractor Hayes Middlesex
- roof repairs Hayes
- roofing company UB3

Goal:

Capture brand discovery and high-intent local commercial searches.

### Cluster 2: Core Service Intent

Target pages:

- Services overview
- individual service pages

Target keyword themes:

- flat roofing Hayes
- pitched roofing Middlesex
- commercial roofing West London
- roof inspections Hayes
- guttering maintenance Hayes

Goal:

Capture users looking for a specific roofing service, often with immediate enquiry intent.

### Cluster 3: Specialist Commercial Intent

Target pages:

- service detail pages for commercial and specialist services

Target keyword themes:

- metal roofing services Hayes
- exterior cladding services Hayes
- rooflight replacement Middlesex
- roof access safety systems West London
- environmental roofing contractor

Goal:

Capture more specialist commercial and envelope-related searches that support higher-value work.

### Cluster 4: Informational / Blog Intent

Target pages:

- blog listing
- blog articles

Target keyword themes:

- roof repair or replacement Hayes
- signs roof needs inspection Hayes
- flat roof maintenance West London
- gutter maintenance advice Middlesex

Goal:

Support informational search, topical authority, and assisted conversion.

### Cluster 5: Trust / Brand Intent

Target pages:

- About
- Contact
- Footer / company details

Target keyword themes:

- about roofing company Hayes
- contact roofer Hayes
- roofing company Middlesex contact

Goal:

Strengthen brand legitimacy and help users who want reassurance before enquiring.
